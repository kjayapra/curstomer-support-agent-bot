# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Password Reset
Customers can reset their password by opening Settings, selecting Security,
and clicking "Reset Password". A reset link will be sent to the verified email.

## Billing and Invoices
Invoices are available in Account > Billing. Customers can download PDFs for any
billing cycle from the invoices list.

## Plan Changes
Plan upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing
cycle. To change plans, go to Account > Plans.

## Shipping Times
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days after processing. Expedited shipping
takes 1-2 business days. International delivery takes 7-14 business days.

## Refund Policy
Refunds are handled by a specialist for security reasons. Gather the order ID
and escalate.

## Account Security
If a customer reports account takeover or unauthorized access, escalate to a
human agent immediately and recommend enabling 2FA.
